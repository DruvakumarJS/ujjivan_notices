Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has issued instructions to banks on the facilities offered to customers who are old / sick / incapacitated and are not willing to open or operate joint accounts

Customers who are old / sick / incapacitated fall under two categories:

  1. A customer who is too old or ill to sign a cheque or cannot be physically present in the bank to withdraw money from his or her account, but can put his or her thumb impression on the cheque or withdrawal form.
  2. A customer who is unable to be physically present in the bank and is also unable to put his or her thumb impression on the cheque or withdrawal form due to certain physical incapacities.
