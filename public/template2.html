As per amended Income Tax Regulations (April 01, 2010), TDS (Tax deducted at Source) would be deducted at a higher rate than current, in case PAN has not been submitted by the deductee to the deductor.
Further it is also mandatory to furnish PAN on Form no. 15H / 15G submitted by the customers. You are therefore requested to submit your PAN copy for updating Bank Records at the earliest to avoid TDS deduction.
